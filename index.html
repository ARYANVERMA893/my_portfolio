Publications

Replacing Human Workers with Robots in Hazardous and Deadly Chemical Factory Environments

The dangerous chemical factory environment poses risks of toxic exposure, extreme thermal conditions, volatile reactions, and invisible airborne hazards to human labor. This paper describes an AI-based robotic system framework to replace human labor in high-risk industrial environments by facilitating safe and reliable autonomous operations under highly constrained and unpredictable conditions. A real-time sensor fusion system with vision, gas, and proximity sensors improves situational awareness to monitor toxic leaks and dynamic hazards in real-time, facilitating adaptive and safe autonomous operation.

Smart Disaster Alert System Powered by Retrieval-Augmented Large Language Models and Real-Time Intelligence Pipelines

This paper proposes a Smart Disaster Alert System that incorporates Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Large Language Model (LLM) designs to provide smart, real-time detection and notification of disasters. The framework utilizes Apache Kafka, PySpark, and AWS S3 for real-time data processing. Integration of RAG search with LLM summarization enables extraction of situational insights and delivery of context-aware alerts, achieving a 40% reduction in response time.
